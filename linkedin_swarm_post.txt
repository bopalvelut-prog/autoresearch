🚀 Just released a "LAN Research Swarm" feature for Andrej Karpathy's autoresearch!

Ever wanted to turn your idle home devices into a distributed AI research cluster?

Inspired by projects like Folding@home, Exo, and prima.cpp, I've built a system where you can:

✅ Run a Coordinator on your main PC (uses a local LLM to generate research ideas)
✅ Run Workers on any laptop/desktop in your house (auto-discovery via mDNS)
✅ Watch them all work together on a real-time Dashboard to optimize your LLM training

It's completely open-source and runs on CPU/Apple Silicon.

Check it out here: https://github.com/bopalvelut-prog/autoresearch
